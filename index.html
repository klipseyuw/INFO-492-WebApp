Agentic AI Systems in Warehouse Logistics Security

Autonomous Threat Detection & Quarantine for Supply Network Resilience

Group 3: Kenta Lipsey, Hanrui Tang, Brian Yuan, Ajit Mallavarapu

Course: INFO 492 B | Date: September 27, 2025

Read Thesis Explore Ecosystem

Thesis Statement

The warehouse logistics industry is highly vulnerable to cyber-physical attacks, ransomware, and third-party supply chain risks. Agentic AI can enhance resilience by autonomously detecting anomalies, quarantining threats, and safeguarding payroll, scheduling, and transportation systems.

Yet, its risk amplification demands robust governance and layered security to prevent cascading disruptions across global supply networks.

Warehouse Vulnerabilities

Cyber-physical attacks, ransomware, and third-party risks threaten payroll, scheduling, and transportation systems

Agentic AI Resilience
